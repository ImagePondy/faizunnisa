Our College

AAS Arts and Science College, Puducherry is the best Engineering College in Puducherry. The prime objective of this institute is to impart quality technical education to all the sectors of the society.

AAS Arts and Science College, is recognized as the best engineering in India and top engineering college in Puducherry. The institute is flourishing with students achievements and placement record with its enormous human and infrastructure resources.

One among the top ten engineering colleges in Tamilnadu is AAS Arts and Science College which is well known for its placement activities due to which students got placed in multinational companies. Students and their parents feel pride to get admission in AAS Arts and Science College.

AAS Arts and Science College is one of the best engineering colleges in Puducherry affiliated to Pondicherry University, Accredited by NAAC with “A” grade, Accredited by NBA – AICTE, New Delhi, an ISO 9001:2000 certified institution and Accredited by TATA consultancy Services.
